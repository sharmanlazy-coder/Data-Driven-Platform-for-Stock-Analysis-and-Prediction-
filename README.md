A powerful, full-stack web application designed to empower retail investors with institutional-grade analytics. This platform combines **real-time market data**, **advanced machine learning models**, and **financial theory (CAPM)** to provide actionable stock insights. It also features a complete **virtual trading environment** to practice strategies risk-free.

---

## üöÄ Key Features

### ü§ñ AI-Powered Predictions
* **Multi-Model Forecasting:** Utilizes **XGBoost**, **Random Forest**, and **Linear Regression** to predict future stock prices.
* **Sentiment Analysis:** Generates "Bullish", "Bearish", or "Neutral" market sentiment based on predictive trends.
* **Confidence Scoring:** Provides a confidence level (High/Medium/Low) for every prediction based on model accuracy metrics (MAPE, R¬≤).
* **Index Prediction:** Specialized forecasting for major indices like **NIFTY 50** and **SENSEX**.

### üìä Advanced Technical & Fundamental Analysis
* **Interactive Charts:** Dynamic candlestick charts with zoom/pan capabilities powered by Plotly.js.
* **Technical Indicators:** Real-time calculation of **RSI**, **MACD**, **Bollinger Bands**, **ATR**, and **Moving Averages (SMA/EMA)**.
* **CAPM Analysis:** Calculates **Beta**, **Alpha**, and **Expected Returns** using the Capital Asset Pricing Model to assess risk-adjusted performance.
* **Security Market Line (SML):** Visualizes undervalued vs. overvalued stocks.

### üíº Virtual Trading Simulator (Demo Mode)
* **‚Çπ100,000 Starting Balance:** Practice trading without risking real money.
* **Real-Time Portfolio:** Track live Profit/Loss (P&L) updates based on current market prices.
* **Trade History:** Comprehensive log of all executed Buy/Sell orders with performance tracking.

### üõ†Ô∏è Tools & Utilities
* **Smart Watchlist:** Track your favorite stocks with instant price change alerts.
* **Stock Comparison:** Compare up to 5 stocks side-by-side on metrics like P/E Ratio, Market Cap, and Volatility.
* **IPO Dashboard:** Stay updated with the latest and upcoming Initial Public Offerings.
* **Sector Performance:** Analysis of top-performing market sectors.
